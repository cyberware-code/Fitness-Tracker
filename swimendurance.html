Swim Endurance Session

Goal

Improve endurance, technique, and efficiency by focusing on a few key elements.

Session Structure

8-Week Swim Progression Plan (3x Per Week)

  • 100m Warm-up (Breaststroke)
  • 200m Drill Work (Key Focus)
  • 8 x 100m Front Crawl (Endurance Set)
  • 100m Cool-down

Session 1 (Body Position & Balance)

Side Balance Drill Session

Goal: Improve body position, balance, and streamline efficiency in the water.

Full Session Breakdown

  • Warm-up: 100m Breaststroke – relaxed, focus on breathing
  • Drill Set: 4 x 50m Side Balance Drill
    • Swim on your side, bottom arm extended forward, top arm resting on hip.
    • Head neutral, looking down, turning only to breathe.
    • Kick continuously, keeping body straight and aligned.
    • Switch sides every 25m.
  • Drill Set: 4 x 50m Side Balance with Kickboard
    • Hold a kickboard with bottom hand while maintaining side position.
    • Use gentle flutter kicks to stay streamlined.
    • Switch sides every 25m.
  • Main Set: 8 x 100m Front Crawl
    • Focus on hip-driven rotation and streamlined body position.
    • Maintain smooth, controlled breathing.
  • Cool-down: 100m Choice Stroke – relaxed recovery

Key Focus Areas

  • Body Alignment: Keep hips and shoulders in a straight line.
  • Hip Rotation: Use your core, not just shoulders, to rotate.
  • Breathing Control: Keep it smooth, avoiding excessive head movement.
  • Controlled Kicking: Gentle, consistent kicks to maintain balance.
