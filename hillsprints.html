Hill Sprints Session

Goal

Develop leg power, running efficiency, and endurance.

Duration

~35-40 minutes

Focus

Short, explosive hill sprints with controlled recovery.

Session Breakdown

  • Warm-up: 10 min (6.5-7.5 kph, 1-2% incline)
  • Dynamic Drills: 4 min (Leg swings, high knees)
  • Sprint 1: 30 sec (10.5-11.0 kph, 6-8% incline)
  • Recovery: 90 sec (5-6 kph, 0-2% incline)
  • Cooldown: 5-10 min (6-7 kph, 1-2% incline)

Key Focus Areas

  • Maintain good form: Tall posture, slight lean from the ankles.
  • Adjust Based on Fatigue: Extend recovery if needed.
