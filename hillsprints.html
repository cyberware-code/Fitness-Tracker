Hill Sprints Session

Goal: Develop leg power, running efficiency, and endurance to support sub-30 5K performance.

Duration: ~35-40 minutes

Focus: Short, explosive hill sprints with controlled recovery

Full Session Breakdown

Stage Incline (%) Speed (kph) Duration Notes
Warm-up 1-2% 6.5-7.5 kph 10 min Easy jog, relaxed breathing

Sprint 5 12% 11.0-11.5 kph 30 sec Keep posture strong

Recovery 0-2% 5-6 kph 75 sec Keep moving, control HR
Cooldown 1-2% 6-7 kph 5-10 min Light jog + static stretching

Key Focus Areas

  • Maintain good form: Stay tall, slight lean from the ankles (not hips).
  • Use short, powerful strides and drive with arms.
  • Avoid bouncing: quick ground contact time is key.

Adjust Based on Fatigue

  • If struggling with HR recovery, extend recovery to 90 sec.
  • If feeling strong, slightly increase speed (but maintain form).
